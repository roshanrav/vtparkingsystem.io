Hokie Parking System

Team: Francis Kwami Adzraku, Joseph Mecherikunnel, Roshan Ravindran, Daodao Zhou

Virginia Tech serves a large community of over 38,000 students and more than 2,500 faculty members. Managing parking efficiently in such a large community is crucial for campus accessibility. Currently, the existing mobile parking app, ParkMobile, has limitations, including not providing real-time information on parking spot availability and allowing users to reserve parking spaces in advance. This limitation can cause inconvenience for students, faculty, staff, and visitors to campus resources.

Our proposed parking system will go beyond the limitations of ParkMobile by providing a more comprehensive and tailored parking solution for the Virginia Tech campus.
