Requirements

Functional Requirements

  • FR-1: The system must support the ability to display real-time availability of parking spots on campus for drivers.
  • FR-2: The system must support the ability to display real-time availability of off-campus parking spots during peak times when no spots are available on campus.
  • FR-3: The system must support the ability to allow drivers to reserve a parking space in advance, with their desired time and location.
  • FR-4: The system must support the ability to provide filtering options for drivers, allowing them to search for parking spots based on location, price, distance to a specific building, or spot size.
  • FR-5: The system must support the ability to allow drivers to rate and leave comments on each parking location.
  • FR-6: The system must support the ability to generate personalized recommendations for parking spots based on previous user ratings and comments.
  • FR-7: The system must support the ability to provide operators with a weekly analysis summary of parking usage, highlighting trends and occupancy rates.

Non-Functional Requirements

  • NFR-1: The system shall update parking availability in real-time with a maximum delay of 5 seconds.
  • NFR-2: The system shall be in full operating mode within two seconds of the application being turned on.
  • NFR-3: The system shall provide filtering and search results within two seconds.
  • NFR-4: The system shall maintain a weekly uptime of 99.9%, ensuring high reliability for users and operators.
  • NFR-5: The system shall generate the weekly analysis summary in a format that is accessible and easy to understand for operators, such as charts or visual summaries.
